Sunny Daycare

Our Registration Requirements Are:

  • Children cannot be younger than six weeks or older than five years.
  • Our fee is $20 per hour.
  • Final pick ups are at 6pm anything past closing time is an addition 10% charge per hour over.
  • If you are 15 minutes late or over a full hour fee will be charged.
  • If you are late on making your payments there will be an addition 20% charge added to your bill.
